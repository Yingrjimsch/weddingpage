Japan

Our trip starts in Japan. For four weeks we will discover the customs and habits of the country.

Of course that includes the amazing city of Tokio, where we will spend a couple of weeks, to enjoy karaoke, sleep in a capsule and see what this great city is all about.

Next stop will be Kyoto, where we will plunge into the japanese culture, visit the temples and the well known old city.

There's much more you'll find on our ToDo list of japan, for example sleeping in a Ryokan or a trip to Okinawa, the island where people get real old.
